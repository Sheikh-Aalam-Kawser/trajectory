# Design System - Trajectory

## Typography
- **Primary / Body**: Inter (sans-serif)
- **Display / Headings**: Space Grotesk (sans-serif)
- **Technical / Status**: JetBrains Mono (monospace)

## Theme & Color Palette
- **Primary**: Indigo (Vibrant Indigo for actionable elements and accentuating transitions)
- **Neutral**: Slate (Sophisticated grays for canvas backgrounds and borders)
- **Accents**: Emerald (Success states and completion badges), Amber (Warnings and adaptation indicators)
